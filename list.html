    • Bulbasaur (001)

    Type Grass / Poison
    Species Seed Pokémon
    Height 0.7 m (2'04")
    Weight 6.9 kg (15.2 lbs.)

    Description
    Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison type Pokémon introduced in Generation 1. It is known as the Seed Pokémon. Bulbasaur resembles a small amphibian, but bears three claws on each of its feet and has no tail. It also has large, red eyes and small, sharp teeth. Its skin is a light, turquoise color with dark, green spots. It has three claws on all four of its legs. Bulbasaur has a "bulb" on its back that grows steadily larger as it matures.

    Charmander (004)

    Type Fire
    Species Lizard Pokémon
    Height 0.6 m (2’00”)
    Weight 8.5 kg (18.7 lbs.)

    Description
    Charmander is a Fire type Pokémon introduced in Generation 1. It is known as the Lizard Pokémon. Charmander is a small, bipedal, dinosaur-like Pokémon. Most of its body is colored orange, while its underbelly is a pale light-yellow color. Charmander, like its evolved forms, has a flame that constantly burns on the end of its tail. The power of Charmander's flame attacks can be gauged by the size of the flame on its tail.

    Squirtle (007)

    Type Water
    Species Tiny Turtle Pokémon
    Height 0.5 m (1’08”)
    Weight 9.0 kg (19.8 lbs.)

    Description
    Squirtle is a Water type Pokémon introduced in Generation 1. It is known as the Tiny Turtle Pokémon. Squirtle is a small, light-blue Pokémon with an appearance similar to a turtle. With its aerodynamic shape and grooved surface, Squirtle's shell helps it wade through the water very quickly. Its shell is highly sturdy, so it can hide within its shell to protect itself from physical attacks.

    Chikorita (152)

    Type Grass
    Species Leaf Pokémon
    Height 0.9 m (2’11”)
    Weight 6.4 kg (14.1 lbs.)

    Description
    Chikorita is a Grass type Pokémon introduced in Generation 2. It is known as the Leaf Pokémon. Chikorita is a small dinosaur-like Pokémon, particularly resembling a Sauropoda. It is a light green color and its eyes are dull red. Around its neck are tiny buds that have the ability to shoot out into long vines. Chikorita are docile and friendly, spending a lot of time in the sun to soak up the sun's rays.

    Height 0.5 m (1’08”)

    Description

    Totodile (158)

    Type Water
    Species Big Jaw Pokémon
    Height 0.6 m (2’00”)
    Weight 9.5 kg (20.9 lbs.)

    Description
    Totodile is a Water type Pokémon introduced in Generation 2. It is known as the Big Jaw Pokémon. Totodile is a small Pokémon that appears as a bipedal crocodile. While most of a Totodile's body is blue, they have a yellow, V-shaped marking across their chest, red eyes and spikes that go down its back to its tail. Totodile are very energetic, cheerful and rowdy, and occasionally can be seen running through swampy areas, biting and chomping at everything that moves around them.

    Description

    Mudkip (258)

    Type Water
    Species Mud Fish Pokémon
    Height 0.4 m (1’04”)
    Weight 7.6 kg (16.8 lbs.)

    Description
    Mudkip is a Water type Pokémon introduced in Generation 3. It is known as the Mud Fish Pokémon. Mudkip is a small, quadrupedal amphibious Pokémon that resembles mudskipper fishes and axolotls. It is cyan blue in color, with a light blue neck, underbelly and a tail. Mudkip and members of its evolution family dwell in swamps or other wetlands, deep inside isolated islands, due to their dislike of freshwater lakes and ponds.
